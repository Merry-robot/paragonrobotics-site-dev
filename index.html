Introduction

We are a FIRST Robotics Competition team,
and a registered 501(c)(3).

The Details

Our Team

Build

Our build team is responsible for the physical construction of our robot. They work with many materials and utilize 3D printing and CNC machining.

Programming

Our programming team is responsible for the code of the robot. They work with the programming language Java.

Buisness & Media

Our buisness and media team is responsible for logo design, shirt design, marketing, and website upkeep. They also design our buttons that we give out during each competition.

Drive Team

The drive team is a side team and is only utilized during the competition season. Anyone from the other subteams can be a part of the drive team. The drive team typically consists of a driver, an operatior, a human player, and a coach. During some seasons, different positions have been added or removed.
